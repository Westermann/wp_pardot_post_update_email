Default Text Mail Template Replace this text with what you would like your email to look like For sending out pardot emails, you are required to include the normal %%unsubscribe%% or %%email_preferences_center%% links as in any other pardot email template. On top of those standard variables you can add custom ones that are fed with information from the wordpress post: %%post_title%% will be populated with the post's header line %%post_content%% will be populated with the first paragraph or first 255 characters of the post content %%post_link%% will be populated with a link to the Wordpress post %%post_image_link%% will be populated with the link to the featured image
